Sole Taps

Alternate light taps on top of the ball with the sole. Warm-up / feel for the ball.

1 min

How to run the circuit

Perform each drill for the listed time/reps, resting ~15–20s between drills. That's roughly 12–13 minutes for one full round. Repeat 2–3 rounds, alternating lead foot each round. Keep touches soft and eyes up between touches once each move feels comfortable.
