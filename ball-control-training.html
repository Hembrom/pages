Mat Soccer Drill

Ball Control Circuit

11 Drills for a Training Mat

1

Sole Taps

Alternate light taps on top of the ball with the sole. Warm-up / feel for the ball.

1 min
2

Sole Rolls

Roll the ball side to side under the sole of one foot at a time.

30s × 2
3

Inside Taps

Quick alternating taps using the inside of each foot, ball stays close.

30s × 2
4

Inside Push

Push the ball from one inside foot to the other, building rhythm.

30s × 2
5

Inside Push (2nd set)

Repeat inside push, focus on quicker tempo / opposite lead foot.

30s × 2
6

Inside / Outside

Inside touch then outside touch of the same foot, moving the ball forward.

30s × 3
7

Inside V-Cuts

Cut the ball in a V-shape using the inside of the foot, changing direction sharply.

30s × 3
8

L Drags

Drag the ball across your body with the sole in an L-shaped path.

30s × 2
9

Stepovers

Circle foot over the top of the ball without touching it, then push off the other way.

30s × 2
10

Croquetas

Quick drag from one foot to the other across the body, low and controlled.

30s × 2
11

Single Leg Juggle (R)

Keep the ball up using only the right foot, controlled low bounces.

50×

How to run the circuit

Perform each drill for the listed time/reps, resting ~15–20s between drills. That's roughly 12–13 minutes for one full round. Repeat 2–3 rounds, alternating lead foot each round. Keep touches soft and eyes up between touches once each move feels comfortable.
